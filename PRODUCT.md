# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Founders and executive teams at small federal contractors (including emerging 8(a) and growth-stage firms) navigating federal procurement, subcontracting, compliance, and organizational scaling.

## Product Purpose

Pazant Consulting Services communicates credibility, governance expertise, and institutional readiness to prospective advisory clients. Success means visitors understand the practice's positioning, trust the founder's credentials, and take action to book a consultation.

## Brand Personality

Executive, grounded, selective, governance-forward. Calm confidence over hype.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages, cream-and-navy AI slop, gradient text, hero metric dashboards, identical icon-card grids, marketing buzzwords, side-stripe card accents, uppercase eyebrows on every section.

## Design Principles

1. Institutional clarity over decoration: structure and copy carry authority.
2. Governance-forward positioning: systems and readiness, not shortcuts.
3. Selective engagement: high-trust, executive-level tone.
4. Human-centered narrative beneath crisp federal-market positioning.
5. Accessible, readable contrast on every surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast for body and heading text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Keyboard-navigable header and CTAs. Semantic HTML and ARIA on interactive components.
